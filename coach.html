KnightSight Coach Dashboard

Powerful tools to support every coach and every student.

What Coaches Can Do

  • Create student accounts or connect with existing users.
  • Assign specific drills to individual students or groups.
  • Choose drill type, difficulty tier, and quantity.
  • View detailed results of completed drills, including accuracy and speed.
  • Track student progress over time through visual analytics.
  • Send notes and feedback on assignments.
  • Export progress summaries for offline review.
